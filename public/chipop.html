Chicken Popcorn

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken pieces
  • Salt 3/4 tsp
  • Pepper 3/4 tsp
  • Buttermilk 1 cup
  • All purpose flour 1 cup
  • Paprika
  • Red Chilli powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Oregano
  • Baking powder

Method of Preperation

  1. Wash and pat dry the chicken fillets/boneless pieces.Cut them into small bite size pieces.
  2. Add these pieces into a bowl. Add salt, pepper powder and pour the buttermilk over it.
  3. Mix it well. Set aside for an hour.
  4. Take a square glass bowl/baking dish and pour the flour/maida. Add all items indicated and mix it well.
  5. Now take out the marinated chicken pieces one at a time and drop them in the flour mixture. Once 5/6 are placed, dredge them well with the flour mixture and keep them side by side on a plate. Repeat in batches of 5/6.
  6. Once the oil is medium hot, drop the breaded chicken pieces one at a time and fry in batches, to not crowd the pan.
  7. Cook until golden brown
  8. Chicken Popcorn is ready to serve
