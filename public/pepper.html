Chicken Pepper

Ingredients

  • 750 grams chicken
  • 5 tsp corn flour
  • 2 tsp maida
  • 3 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • salt as per your taste
  • coconut oil
  • 5 long green chillies
  • 1 strand of curry leaves
  • 1 tsp lemon or vinegar

Method of Preperation

  1. Marination: Clean the chicken well and marinate it for an hour after adding ginger garlic paste+salt+proper+corn flour+maida+lemon.
  2. fry the chicken in coconut oil.
  3. another pan add oil, then add curry leaves and green chillies.
  4. let it cook for a while say 2-3 mins until it turns crispy.
  5. Add in the fried chicken and sprinkle some pepper.
  6. Let it cook for 5 mins and then you may turn off the stove.
  7. Your chicken pepper is ready to be served
