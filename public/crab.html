Crab Masala

Ingredients

  • Peppercorns
  • Cumin seeds
  • Fennel seeds
  • Garlic 10 nos Chopped
  • Chopped ginger
  • Water
  • Crab 400 gms
  • Gingelly Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Onion - 4 nos thinly Sliced
  • Green Chilli - 2 nos
  • Ground Masala Paste
  • Turmeric powder
  • Chilli Powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Tomato 1 chopped
  • Curry leaves

Method of Preperation

  1. To a mixer jar, add peppercorns, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, garlic, ginger. Grind it
  2. Add some Water and grind it fine paste
  3. Heat a pan with oil. Add onions and saute for 2 mins
  4. Add green chillies, curry leaves and saute along
  5. Once the onions have turned to a golden color, add the ground paste.
  6. Add salt, turmeric powder, chilli powder, coriander powder and mix well.
  7. Add tomato and saute for 5 mins
  8. Add the cleaned and cut crabs and mix well
  9. Add water and mix well, Cook for 10 mins
  10. Finally add some curry leaves and serve it hot.
