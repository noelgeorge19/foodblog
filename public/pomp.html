Pomfret Masala

Ingredients

  • Fish (Mackerel/King fish/Pomfret/Lady fish) ½ kg
  • Red chilli powder 2 tsp or as per taste
  • Turmeric powder ½ tsp
  • Lime juice 2 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Byadgi or kashmiri dry red chillies 6 to 7
  • Onion 1 small size
  • Tomato 1 small size
  • Garlic 3 flakes
  • Cumin seeds 1 tsp
  • Vinegar 1 tbsp
  • Curry leaves few
  • Coconut oil 2 tbsp + 2tbsp
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing

Method of Preperation

  1. Cut and clean any fish of your choice such as mackerels, king fish, lady fish or pomfret. Wash them and drain completely.
  2. Prepare a paste for marinating the fish and for that add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, lime juice and salt in a mixing bowl.Add water in little quantities till you prepare thick fine paste.
  3. Apply this paste all over the fish and set aside for half an hour
  4. Meanwhile prepare for the masala paste to coat the fish after frying. Using a blender blend all the ingredients mentioned under “for the masala” to a fine paste.
  5. Now heat a skillet for shallow frying the fish. Sprinkle 2 tbsp of coconut oil and when it receives required heat place marinated fish one by one
  6. Fry both sides of the fish evenly and when they are cooked remove onto a separate plate.
  7. Do not turn off the flame and continue cooking further on the same skillet.
  8. Further add 2 tbsp of coconut oil again and add curry leaves and finely chopped onion.
  9. When onion turn golden add grounded masala paste and salt.
  10. You can add little quantity of water if it is too thick. Cook further till raw smell disappears and oil will leave from sides.
  11. .Now place fried fish between the skillet and coat well with masala. Cook further for 3 to 4 minutes till fish absorb the masala flavor
  12. Your fish masala fry is ready to serve
