Chicken Tikka

Ingredients

  • 750 grams chicken
  • Salt to taste
  • 1.5 nos Lemon
  • 1/2 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 7.5 tsp Mustard oil
  • 1.5 tbsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • 1.5 cup Curd
  • Chat Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Methi leaves Powder

Method of Preperation

  1. Pat dry the chicken and mic it with sprinkle salt, lemon juice, ginger and garlic paste.Keep it aside for an hour.
  2. Meanwhile pour mustard oil in a fresh bowl and add Kashmiri chilli powder to it. Stir and add thick curd, lemon uice,salt,ginger paste, garlic paste and methi leaves powder.Mix it and keep aside
  3. Now squeeze netween your palm the chicken to remove and excess moisture.
  4. Place the chicken pieces in the second marinade mix to coat the chicken evenly.
  5. Heat the grill pan to high. Lightly oil it and place the chicken peices on the pan.
  6. Within 2-3 mins turn over to get grill marks on the other side as well
  7. Your chicken Tikka is ready to be served
